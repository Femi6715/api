Responsible Gaming



Please play responsibly as gaming is supposed to be a form of entertainment!


At Padi Lotto, we are sincerely committed to helping and protecting those who wish to limit the amount they play with or those who should not be playing at all.

If you feel that playing games might be a deterrent to your life in anyway rather than a form of entertainment, or if you suffer any form of mental health issues due to gaming which may include depression, anxiety, or worse, then we encourage you to visit Gamblers Anonymous

From our end, we also afford you the ability to halt yourself from playing for various periods of time. Please send an email to support@padilotto.ng and we will be glad to help you.

Any underage player who has provided false or wrong information regarding their true age may have all winnings forfeited and could face criminal prosecution. If you believe you might have a gambling problem, and seek further advice or counseling, you can visit this website: Gambling Therapy

OUR MEASURES

1. When signing up on our Website, there is a mandatory checkbox for age. Without ticking that the person registering is above 18 years of age, the registration won't be successful and thus, the person cannot play Padi Lotto.

2. We do not target underage players with our marketing and advertising. It is inconsistent with our corporate values to attract underage players.

SELF-EXCLUSION

If you wish to be restricted from being able to play Padi Lotto at any time, simply send us an email via support@padilotto.ng asking to be blocked from accessing our Website. This will be a permanent block and you would have to contact us again if you want to regain access.

PLEASE, ALWAYS PLAY RESPONSIBLY (ONLINE AND OFFLINE)

1. Do not be overly confident that the possible outcome of a game of chance would be in your favour, you can`t be so sure.

2. Always ask yourself: what if I lose? Will I be fine?

3. Play only with the money that you can afford to lose. Never use money that you need for important things such as food, rent, bills, or tuition.

4. Play for fun and entertainment, as playing to make money is neither a safe nor sensible reason to play.
