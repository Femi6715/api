•  HOW TO PLAY
      To participate in a draw, register or login and fund your account. You will get 100% Welcome Bonus on your first deposit and 20% bonus on every other deposit of N1,000 and above!

      Having done that, enter your phone number under a draw category of your choice and click PLAY. Your phone number will generate a Unique Lucky Ticket ID for you.

      Your Lucky Ticket ID qualifies you for the stated winning. Your tickets, the status and other details are available in your Dashboard when you log in.
