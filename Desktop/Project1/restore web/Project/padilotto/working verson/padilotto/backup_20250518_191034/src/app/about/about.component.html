About Padi Lotto



The Nigerian Lottery Industry is male-dominated and one of the reasons is because of the complexity of the available lottery models and the difficulty of winning.

*What if we keep it simple so you don't worry about numbers?
*What if we have a lotto so simple to understand and easy to play with small stakes?
*What if we have a bi-gender friendly lotto that gives every player an equal chance of winning?
*What if we have a lotto where it is guaranteed that there will always be winners in every draw?

To answer the above questions, Simple Touch Applications Limited launches Padi Lotto and all that is needed to play Padi Lotto is a phone number!



When you enter your phone number and click PLAY, a Unique Lucky Ticket ID will be generated for you. This Lucky Ticket ID qualifies you for the stated winning -that's all!

When the draw closes by 6pm, all Ticket IDs generated are shuffled by our Bespoke Lottery Machine and Winning Ticket IDs are selected and published on our Website under "WINNERS".

When you win, your account is credited instantly and you can win multiple times in a draw! Simple, right? We know! PLAY NOW!
