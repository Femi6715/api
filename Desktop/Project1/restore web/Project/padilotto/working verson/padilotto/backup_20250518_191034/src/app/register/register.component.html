How to Play



    1) Register, Log in and fund your account. You'll get 100% Welcome Bonus on your first deposit and a 20% bonus on other deposits of N1,000 and above.


    2) Click on your preferred draw (N2,500 | N5,000 | N10,000).


    3) Enter your phone number (11 digits) and click "PLAY"


    4) You will get a Unique Lucky Ticket ID which qualifies you for the winning.


    5) Check your Ticket ID and status in your dashboard. "Pending" status means the draw is still on and winners have not emerged.


    6) Our Bespoke Lottery Machine picks Winning Ticket IDs and publishes them within 30-60 minutes after draws close on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays (11:45pm). Check the "WINNERS" tab for results.


    Note: You can win multiple times in a draw if you play multiple times! Register and Log in to PLAY NOW!
