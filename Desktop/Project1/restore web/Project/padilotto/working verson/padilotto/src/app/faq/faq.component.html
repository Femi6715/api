Is Padi Lotto like sports betting?


- No, Padi Lotto has nothing to do with sports, neither is it the regular 1-90 lotto model. You don’t need to pick numbers to play Padi Lotto; all you need to play & win is your phone number.

What am I betting on?


- You aren’t betting on anything. Padi Lotto is similar to a raffle. To win, you need a Ticket ID. To get a Ticket ID, you need your phone number; that’s all.

Must I use my phone number?


- You can use any phone number (11 digits). The phone number used does not determine your chances, it is your Lucky Ticket ID that does. Our Bespoke Lottery Machine picks winners using Ticket IDs.

Are you sure I will win?


- If you play, you can win, if you don’t play, you cannot win. Our guarantee is that winners emerge in every draw and you could be one of them. Play Now!

How will I know if I win?


- Check on the “Winners” tab on the website to see if your Ticket ID appears on the list or check “My Tickets” in your dashboard to see if your ticket status has changed to “Won”. When you win, your Padi Lotto Wallet will be credited instantly with your winnings and you can place withdrawal anytime.

Why is my ticket status showing “pending”?


- When you play in a draw and the draw hasn’t closed, your ticket status will show “pending”. When the draw closes and winners are selected, your ticket status will change to either “Won” or “Lost”.

When will results be out?


- Results are published between 30-60 minutes after a draw closes. To know when a draw will close, please check the date and countdown timer on the draw page.
